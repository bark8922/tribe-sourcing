METHODOLOGY

Numbers reflect internal sourcing work only, what sourcers produced while on the Bench/Internal allocation in BambooHR.

Counted: all sourcing work (any client, including Tribe internal) done during a sourcer's Bench/Internal periods.

Excluded:

  • Work done while a sourcer was allocated to a client (that's client/TA work, not internal sourcing)
  • Onboarding work, if a sourcer's Bench window is short and they sourced for the client they're about to join
  • People in fully-IR roles (Sanja Pavlovikj), they're internal recruiting, not the sourcing team
  • Sourcer-quarters with fewer than 5 contacts kept (noise filter)
