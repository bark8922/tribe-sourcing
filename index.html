METHODOLOGY

Job-based. Each closed role = 1 event. A job is "closed by sourcing" only when (a) at least one hire was made, (b) that hire was sourced by the job's responsible sourcer, and (c) that sourcer is on our 19-roster TS team. "Closed" = archived in Bubble OR (hire made + no activity in 90 days) OR (no activity in 90 days).

Bulk roles (11+ hires on one job, mostly Wolt courier batches) are tagged with a purple chip. The toggle in the rate column controls whether bulk hires factor into the closing-rate calculation.
